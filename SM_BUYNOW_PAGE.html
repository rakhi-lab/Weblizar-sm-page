School Management - Education & Learning Management

School Management is a WordPress plugin to manage one or multiple schools and their entities such as classes, sections, students, exams, ID cards, admit cards, teachers, staff, fees, invoices, income, expense, noticeboard, study materials, staff/teacher can send homework SMS on student/parent mobile numbers and much more.

Your school can take live classes of your students using Zoom. Create unlimited live sessions with our system. You can publish the result, admit cards, certificates publicly without any login; students can get the results using their details.
